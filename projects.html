Markets We Serve

We provide precision-engineered hydraulic cylinders for a wide range of demanding industries, ensuring durability, reliability, and peak performance.

Aerospace

Precision-honed barrels, hydraulic rods, and telescopic cylinder components by Genuine Grinding & Honing Works support reliable performance in aircraft landing gear, jetways, ground handling systems, and baggage equipment. Our custom machining and repair capabilities ensure durability and accuracy for critical aerospace operations.

Construction Equipment

Genuine Grinding & Honing Works delivers rugged, custom-machined hydraulic components built to withstand extreme pressure, heat, shock, vibration, and contamination. Trusted by OEMs and contractors, our cylinders and precision services keep construction equipment running reliably in the toughest environments.

Off-Road Equipment

We have the technical knowledge and production excellence to help off-road equipment deliver top performance under the harshest conditions. In fact, every cylinder we manufacture meets or exceed industry requirements.

Automotive

Our aluminum double-acting and telescopic hydraulic cylinders feature unique designs. Most importantly, they have set the standard for reliability in auto carrier and truck equipment applications.

Marine

Genuine Grinding & Honing Works manufactures and reconditions corrosion-resistant hydraulic components in stainless steel and aluminum for marine applications — from small boat lifts to heavy-duty davits. Our precision work ensures long-lasting performance and reliability in harsh, saltwater environments.

Agriculture

Genuine Grinding & Honing Works provides durable, cost-effective hydraulic components that support long operating life in tractors, harvesters, and other farm machinery. Our precision manufacturing helps OEMs and farmers meet demanding productivity goals with reliable performance in field conditions.

Our Applications

From heavy-duty construction to automotive transport, our cylinders are designed for performance and longevity in a variety of critical applications.

Road Pavement & Planers

Genuine Grinding & Honing Works supplies rugged hydraulic components for road pavers, milling machines, and asphalt planers. Built to handle vibration, dust, and extreme wear, our parts perform reliably under the harshest construction site conditions.

Utility Trucks

Genuine Grinding & Honing Works provides custom hydraulic components for dump bodies, lift gates, utility cranes, and service trucks. From single-acting and double-acting cylinders to telescopic rams, our precision-engineered solutions are built to handle demanding field operations with strength and reliability.

Loaders

This is tough, dirty work and requires a hydraulic cylinders capable of working day after day, year after year. Due to the environment, cylinders must be highly engineered to ensure contamination is not introduced into the functionality.

Excavators

Genuine Grinding & Honing Works manufactures and reconditions hydraulic components for excavators and digging equipment that operate under extreme pressure, vibration, and contamination. Our precision machining and robust builds ensure high-performance cylinders that meet the demanding conditions of the construction and earthmoving industries.

Forklifts

Genuine Grinding & Honing Works delivers high-performance hydraulic components for forklifts and material handling equipment. Our precisely machined cylinders are built for long life and reliable performance, meeting the safety and load-handling demands of high-frequency industrial use.

Mobile Cranes (Truck-Mounted)

We have unsurpassed experience designing custom hydraulic cylinders for every application in the mobile crane industry, giving safety factors the foremost consideration to prevent issues like thermal drifting or rod buckling.

Crawler Cranes

Our heavy-duty hydraulic cylinders are engineered for the immense lifting power and stability required by crawler cranes, ensuring smooth operation and durability on challenging terrains.

Tower Cranes

We manufacture and service precision hydraulic systems for tower crane erection, climbing, and slewing mechanisms, meeting the strict safety and performance standards of high-rise construction.

Telescopic Cranes

Genuine Grinding & Honing Works specializes in multi-stage telescopic cylinders, providing the precise control, strength, and reliability needed for extending and retracting crane booms under heavy loads.

Loader Cranes (Knuckle Boom Cranes)

Our custom-designed cylinders for loader and knuckle boom cranes offer superior articulation and lifting capacity, enabling efficient material handling in tight spaces with exceptional control.

Floating Cranes (Marine Cranes)

Built with corrosion-resistant materials and robust seals, our hydraulic cylinders are designed to withstand harsh marine environments, powering floating cranes for offshore construction and port operations.

Pick and Carry Cranes

We supply compact and powerful hydraulic cylinders for pick and carry cranes, delivering the responsive performance and maneuverability essential for moving loads quickly around job sites.
