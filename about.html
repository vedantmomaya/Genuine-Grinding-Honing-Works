Our Group Companies

We operate a diversified set of engineering and industrial service brands, each with a unique focus and specialization.

Genuine Grinding & Honing Works is a trusted name in hydraulic cylinder component manufacturing, precision job work, and industrial maintenance services. Our expertise includes barrel honing, die grinding, boring, and surface finishing for heavy-duty parts used in construction, mining, and OEM systems. Built on decades of family-driven mechanical experience, we combine traditional craftsmanship with modern tooling to deliver high-accuracy, reliable solutions for mission-critical equipment.

Shree Zolai Engineering Works represents the roots of our family’s engineering legacy. Specializing in manual and semi-automated machining, the company delivers cost-effective solutions for general industrial repairs, custom fabrication, and conventional job work. Supporting local industries and legacy machinery, the workshop continues to operate with the same commitment to reliability and craftsmanship that built our group’s foundation.

Omkar Lifters offers professional crane rental and lifting services across Maharashtra, serving construction, infrastructure, and industrial installation sectors. With a fleet of mobile and telescopic cranes, and experienced operators, we provide safe, timely, and efficient lifting support at project sites. Focused on reliability and on-ground responsiveness, Omkar Lifters adds heavy-lifting capability to our engineering group’s growing list of industrial services.
